The project is a single-page application designed to simulate a login portal. Built using the powerful combination of ReactJS and TailwindCSS, this web application boasts pixel-perfect design accuracy, adapting the original concept from a Figma design. Special emphasis has been placed on its interactive components like hover animations and login form validation messages.


The project underwent thorough testing to ensure its responsiveness across different devices and browsers. Functionality tests were also conducted to guarantee the hover animations, and form validations worked as intended. Any discrepancies from the Figma design were meticulously corrected to maintain design accuracy.


This Magebit test project serves as a testament to the skillset and attention to detail applied to the development process. Leveraging the capabilities of ReactJS and the aesthetic prowess of TailwindCSS, the project embodies a blend of functionality and design excellence.
