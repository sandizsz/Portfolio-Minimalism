The TabuReTe website was developed to provide an online platform for the Association "Taburete" to showcase their services, share information, and engage with their audience. The website was designed in Figma by the Association, and it was created using the WordPress CMS with the help of various technologies and tools. The TabuReTe website was developed with a focus on flexibility and modularity to accommodate future changes and additions. The WordPress CMS, combined with Elementor, offers a user-friendly interface that empowers the Association to make updates and add new information whenever required.
