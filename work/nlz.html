In 2023, a close friend with an agriculture business in Sweden reached out to me, expressing the need for a professional website but unsure about the necessary steps to achieve it. Collaboratively, we formulated a comprehensive plan, conceptualizing the design and determining the website's essential functionalities. With this blueprint at hand, I embarked on the technical execution, leveraging WordPress, CSS, HTML, Advanced Custom Fields (ACF), Custom Post Types (CPT), and SEO optimization.


Setting up the domain, initiating a domain email, securing hosting, and implementing SEO optimization were the preliminary tasks that I handled. In the quest to boost the website's visibility, I not only optimized the website but also created SEO-oriented blog articles. These efforts greatly enhanced the website's reach, securing it a place among the top five positions for several crucial job seeker search queries.


The centerpiece of the SIA NLZ website is its fully responsive design, ensuring seamless navigation regardless of device or screen size. One standout feature is a form for potential employees, which sees an average of five daily submissions. This tool provides my friend with a streamlined process to review the applicants and select suitable workers for his business.


Through the SIA NLZ project, I not only created a visually appealing and functional WordPress theme but also empowered an agriculture business to broaden its reach and streamline its hiring process. The website stands as a testament to the power of effective web development and SEO practices in transforming business operations.
